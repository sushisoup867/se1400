Competitions

We participate in multiple CTFO clubs and competitions such as FBLA, and Cyber Patriot

FBLA

Students in the Computer Science pathway at Career Tech High School use their tech skills in FBLA by competing in events like coding, cybersecurity, and web design, while also building leadership and business knowledge.

Cyber Patriot

Computer Science students at Career Tech High School can join FBLA to compete in tech and business events and also take part in CyberPatriot, where they learn cybersecurity skills by defending virtual networks in team-based challenges.
